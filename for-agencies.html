Partnership For Agencies

A reliable coding partner that delivers solutions to your business challenges and stays by your side in the long term. Having the whole spectrum of web development expertise, we are here to take care of your projects while making it possible for you to focus on growing your own business.

Top Solutions Our Partners Seek

  • Natural Team Extension

    A reliable and trusted long-term partner that will handle your multiple projects while you focus on planning and growing your own business.

  • Jelled Team at Your Disposal

    A strongly knit team of experts, tightly interacting with each other. We’ve got the whole spectrum of web dev expertise: HTML, design, back-end.

  • Dedicated Developer

    A developer who knows your project like the back of their hand, provides daily support, undertakes modifications, implements new functionalities, etc.

Shaping a Great Partnership Step by Step

  1. State Your Challenge and Goals. We Analyze and Come with a Possible Solution.

    Your challenges require unique approaches.

    Maybe you need a trusted team to handle your many ongoing projects. Or look for a jelled team with multi-expertise to take care of your complex project. Perhaps, you search for a dedicated professional to maintain your project on daily basis, or you have a whole ‘nother kind of problem. Whatever your challenge is, we are here to find a working solution.

  2. Pilot Project

    You literally get the chance to test us and see if we are a match.

    We’ll give you the opportunity to evaluate our work via a pilot project and see if we are the right partner for your business. A pilot project could be the coding of 1 homepage, a specific feature, or just the progress we’ve made for 8 working hours. This way you’ll get an idea about the quality of our work, the communication process, and everything else that is essential to you.

  3. Collaboration and Bonding

    Trust and smooth workflow are of paramount importance to us and to you, we believe. Once we start collaborating, both our teams will spend some time jelling and adjusting in order to develop a strong and healthy B2B relationship.

  4. Evaluation and Adapting

    Industries, technologies and business needs are rapidly evolving these days. What every partnership needs in order to work out, is constantly assess the changes and adapt in a timely manner.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are the questions that you ask the most. If you can’t find the answer you’re looking for, buzz us.

  • How do we communicate?

    The project correspondence goes through our custom built management system. The system keeps records of all projects, communication history, and full payment history. Phone calls and skype meetings are also fine.

    Are there any contracts?

    It’s up to you. We are perfectly fine with either signing a contract, or not. We know that formal contracts don’t make successful relationships, people do.

    Do you work under a non-disclosure agreement?

    Sure, this is the standard. We are absolutely fine signing an NDA.

  • Will I have a dedicated account manager?

    All your projects will be assigned to the same account manager. Your account manager will get to know your team, the projects you submit and will be able to handle your requests in the most time-efficient manner.

    Can we negotiate an accountant-friendly payment policy?

    Of course, just say the word. Fortnightly, monthly… whatever suits your needs.
