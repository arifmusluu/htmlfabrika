How We Make htmlBurger

Transparency is one of the principles we abide by. This is how our service works.

  1. Submit Your Order

    The whole process gets triggered when you submit your order. We will also expect your corresponding design files (PSD, Sketch, Adobe XD, PNG, Ai, JPG, Indd, etc.) and your thorough instructions.

  2. Discuss the Details

    Once we receive your order, a technically experienced manager will shortly get back to you to discuss everything essential about your project and clear up any ambiguity.

  3. Actual Coding

    As soon as we get on the same page, we are ready to proceed with the coding part. The manager transfers your project to a developer who hand codes it by strictly following your instructions and our coding standards.

  4. QA and Testing

    After the project coding is complete, a quality assurance specialist thoroughly goes through it to check if everything works properly. They also make sure if the code is written according to our coding standards and your instructions.

  5. Fast First Preview

    Once the QA specialist has done all the testing and double-checked that the code is bug free, we will provide you with a preview. Then we wait for your approval or further instructions if you need anything changed.

  6. Fine-Tuning

    Almost there! In case you have any changes or fixes, we apply them at this step. We carefully follow your feedback instructions and make sure you are completely happy with the outcome.

  7. You Get the Source Files

    When the final result has fully met (or even gone beyond) your expectations, we will provide you with a payment link. After payment completion, you receive full access to all source files and our life-time support.

And That's Pretty Much It. Your Project is Ready!

We care about your reliance. In case anything stops working properly in the future, do not hesitate to get in touch. We will fix all code bugs for free, no matter how much time has passed after your project completion.
